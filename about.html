About RecipeFinder

Our Mission

RecipeFinder was created to help home cooks discover new and exciting recipes from around the world. Our goal is to make cooking accessible to everyone, from beginners to experienced chefs.

We source our recipes from TheMealDB API, which provides thousands of delicious meal ideas.
